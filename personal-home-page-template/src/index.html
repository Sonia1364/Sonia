About me

Hello I am Sonia, A multimedia designer. Welcome to my mini portfolio website, My name is Sonia, and most of my friends call me SONI for short. I am a multimedia designer and currently staying in Manchester. I am a person who has a high passion for design technologies, art, and illustration. One of my dreams is to master all the design technologies and become one of the TOP designers in the world, and now, I am working toward it. Since I was a child, I have loved to draw, and this hobby has led me into the design field. I found myself especially interested in accessible website design ever since I learned about the W3C web standard (the year 2007 ). Nowadays, I spend most of my free time researching web design technologies. I am good at constructing standard compliance XHTML web site and interactive flash applications. Hence, I have prepared myself to accept the challenges in this ever-changing world, and I will try my best to become a TOP designer. That could be me in a few years :-)
